DOLLAR$ & $EN$E

Because of several factors (ie: economy, interest rates, jobs, and supply and demand), the housing market has made it near impossible for some people to buy a house on their own.

The median age of first time-home buyers was 33 years old in 2021 and has now risen to 36.

In general, about 66% of first-time home buyers are white, 14% Hispanic, 11% Asian American and 9% black.

DOLLAR$ & $EN$E is a financial expense tracker application aimed to help first-time minority buyers learn how to budget and save to be ready for when it's time to put a down-payment on their first house.
